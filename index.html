Who we are

Innovation, integrity and excellence

Those are the pillars and values of Advanced Network Technologies. We embrace change and welcome new technology. We thrive on flexibility and take pride in our ability to adapt to new approaches.

Advanced Network Technologies invests in and manages telecom and IT projects, with special focus on fibre deployments, 5G and mobile services and advanced private connectivity projects.

What we do

Services

We provide strategic and operational consultancy and invest in IT and telecoms projects.

+ Strategy

Technology strategy and business strategy are key for telecoms. We have experience on both the demand and supply sides and we can help with new technology assessments and business cases; investment strategies and capital planning for next-generation networks and technologies; strategic technology deployment and adoption programme management; Fibre rollouts, 5G and mobile services, and IT service rationalisation and transformation.

+ Plan

From upgrading access networks to planning, designing and building greenfield, we’ve done it all. Working with us, you’ll get an always-available team to help you understand your options, where you’ll get the biggest impact, and how to put it all into practice. We bring next-gen access networks closer, every day. We draw on holistic network excellence to deliver better access: a team that puts your business goals first, expert project management from a talented team and an automated toolchain throughout your project.

+ Manage

We help our customers establish a clear, actionable, and quantified perspective on which capabilities they should prioritize to build innovative products and services, and digitize their processes for greater effectiveness. We create road maps that detail the business requirements to realise value from these new digital opportunities. Our distinctive approach enables telecom companies to focus on their greatest strengths and gain a competitive advantage.

+ Invest

With so many digital possibilities, it’s critical to invest in the right ones. We are a fast growing, entrepreneurial investment manager based in the City of London. We believe a great investment opportunity isn’t just financial, it’s a chance to invest in things that matter: our environment, our health, our society, our local communities and our economy. Our ability to adapt and innovate to create investment products for our customers is key to our growth and success and our core values are vital to everything we do, from the businesses we invest in to the people we hire.
